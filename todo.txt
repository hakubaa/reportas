Fields specification:
- type: point in time (sc. pit) / period of time (sc. pot); 
  balance sheet's fields are given at certain point in time, net and loss
  account and cash flow statments' fields show company performance over some
  period of time

- time range (in case of period of time's fields): year, quarter, half a year;
  one should specify end of the period and type e.g: quarter 2015-03-31 means
  that the data concerns first quarter of 2015; all data are given at the end
  of the months so 2015-03 is enough: q - quarter, h - half-a-year, y - year,
  q201503, y201512, m201506, q201512
